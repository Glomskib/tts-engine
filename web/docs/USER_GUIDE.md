## Skit Generator

The Skit Generator is your main tool for creating video scripts.

### How to Use

1. **Select a Product**: Choose from your product catalog or enter details manually
2. **Set Intensity**:
   - **Safe (1-3)**: Educational, informative content
   - **Balanced (4-6)**: Engaging with personality
   - **Edgy (7-10)**: Bold, attention-grabbing hooks
3. **Choose a Template** (optional): Start with proven formats
4. **Generate**: Click the button and wait for AI magic

### Understanding Your Script

Each generated skit includes:

- **Hook Line**: The attention-grabbing opener (first 1-3 seconds)
- **Beats**: Scene-by-scene breakdown with timing
- **Dialogue**: What to say in each scene
- **On-Screen Text**: Text overlays to add
- **B-Roll Suggestions**: Supplementary footage ideas
- **CTA**: Call-to-action at the end

### Tips for Better Results

1. **Add product benefits**: The more context, the better the script
2. **Use audience personas**: Scripts match your target audience's voice
3. **Try different intensities**: Same product, different vibes
4. **Score your scripts**: Use AI scoring to identify winners

### Saving Scripts

- Click "Save to Library" to keep scripts for later
- Add tags and notes for organization
- Mark favorites with the star icon

## Audience Personas

Build detailed profiles of your target customers to generate more authentic content.

### Creating a Persona

1. Go to **Audience** in the sidebar
2. Click "New Persona"
3. Fill in the details:
   - **Name**: "Stressed Mom", "Skeptical Buyer", etc.
   - **Demographics**: Age, gender, lifestyle
   - **Tone**: How they communicate
   - **Phrases They Use**: Actual words they say
   - **Pain Points**: Their struggles
   - **Objections**: Common hesitations

### Using Personas in Generation

When generating skits, select a persona to:
- Match their communication style
- Address their specific pain points
- Use language they relate to
- Handle their objections naturally

### Best Practices

- Base personas on real customer research
- Include actual quotes from reviews/comments
- Update personas as you learn more
- Create 3-5 distinct personas per product category

## Pain Points Extraction

Extract authentic customer language from reviews and comments.

### How to Extract

1. Go to **Audience** â†’ **Pain Points** tab
2. Paste customer reviews, comments, or feedback
3. Click "Extract Pain Points"
4. Review the extracted insights:
   - Pain points with intensity levels
   - How customers describe their problems
   - Common objections
   - Language patterns

### What Gets Extracted

- **Pain Points**: Core problems customers mention
- **How They Describe It**: Exact phrases used
- **Emotional State**: Frustrated, hopeful, desperate, etc.
- **Intensity**: Low, medium, high, extreme
- **Frequency**: How often this appears

### Sources to Use

- Amazon product reviews
- TikTok comments on competitor videos
- Reddit discussions
- Customer support tickets
- Survey responses

### Adding to Library

Click "Add to Library" to save extracted pain points for use in script generation.

## Tips & Best Practices

### For Better Scripts

1. **Be specific with products**: Include benefits, not just features
2. **Use real customer language**: Extract from actual reviews
3. **Match your audience**: Select the right persona
4. **Iterate**: Generate multiple versions, pick the best

### For Content Strategy

1. **Build a persona library**: Cover your main audience segments
2. **Track what works**: Use the rating system
3. **Study winners**: Analyze successful competitor content
4. **Test intensities**: Same product, different energy levels

### For Efficiency

1. **Use templates**: Start with proven formats
2. **Save favorites**: Build your own template library
3. **Batch generate**: Create multiple scripts at once
4. **Use keyboard shortcuts**: `Cmd/Ctrl + G` for quick access

## Troubleshooting

### Script Generation Failed

- Check your credit balance
- Ensure product details are filled in
- Try a different intensity level
- Contact support if persists

### Extraction Not Working

- Paste more text (minimum 50 characters)
- Ensure text contains customer language
- Check for special characters

### Can't Save Scripts

- Check your internet connection
- Ensure you're logged in
- Clear browser cache
