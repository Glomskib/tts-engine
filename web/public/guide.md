# FlashFlow AI — User Guide

Everything you need to start making TikTok Shop content with FlashFlow.

---

## 1. Getting Started

### First Login
1. Go to your FlashFlow URL and click **Sign Up** (or **Log In** if you already have an account)
2. Use your email + password or sign in with Google
3. You'll land on the **Dashboard** — this is your home base

### Adding Your First Products
1. Click **Products** in the left sidebar (under MANAGEMENT)
2. Click **Add Product** in the top right
3. Fill in:
   - **Name**: The exact product name (e.g., "Ice Roller for Face & Eyes")
   - **Brand**: The brand name
   - **Category**: Beauty, Health, Supplements, Wellness, etc.
   - **Description**: What the product does, key benefits, who it's for. The more detail, the better your AI scripts will be!
4. Click Save

**Shortcut: Bulk Import**
- Go to **Import Products** in the sidebar
- Paste TikTok Shop product URLs (one per line)
- FlashFlow will scrape the product info automatically

<!-- Screenshot: Products page with a few products listed -->

### Creating Audience Personas
1. Go to **Audiences** in the sidebar
2. Click **Create Persona**
3. Describe your target viewer:
   - Name (give them a real name, like "Tired Mom Sarah")
   - Age range, lifestyle, pain points
   - Phrases they use, words to avoid
   - What content they engage with
4. This helps the AI write scripts that speak directly to your audience

## 2. Content Studio — Generating Scripts

This is where the magic happens.

### Quick Generate (Easiest Way)
1. Go to **Content Studio**
2. At the top, you'll see **Quick Generate** presets:
   - **Trending Hook** — viral hook pattern
   - **Pain Point Skit** — relatable problem, product solves it
   - **Before/After** — transformation content
   - **Testimonial** — customer story style
   - **Unboxing** — first reaction content
   - **Day in Life** — lifestyle integration
3. Select a product, then click any preset
4. Done! Your script is generated

<!-- Screenshot: Quick Generate presets panel -->

### Full Generate (More Control)
1. Choose a **Product** from the dropdown
2. Pick a **Content Type** (skit, story, testimonial, etc.)
3. Adjust settings if you want:
   - **Risk Level**: Safe (clean) → Balanced → Spicy (edgy humor)
   - **Target Duration**: Quick (15s), Standard (30s), Extended (60s)
   - **Hook Strength**: How aggressive the opening is
4. Click **Generate**
5. Review the script — the AI will show you:
   - Hook line
   - Scene-by-scene beats with timing
   - Dialogue to say
   - On-screen text overlays
   - CTA (call to action)

### Tips for Better Scripts
- **Add detail to your products** — the AI can only work with what you give it
- **Use audience personas** — scripts with personas feel more authentic
- **Try multiple variations** — generate 3 at once and pick the best
- **Save good ones** — click Save to Library so you don't lose them

## 3. Pipeline — From Script to Posted

The Pipeline tracks every video from idea to published.

### Status Flow
```
SCRIPTED → ASSIGNED → IN_PROGRESS → REVIEW → APPROVED → POSTED
```

1. **SCRIPTED**: Script is written, needs to be assigned to someone to film/edit
2. **ASSIGNED**: Given to a VA (video editor) to work on
3. **IN_PROGRESS**: VA is actively editing
4. **REVIEW**: VA submitted their work, needs your approval
5. **APPROVED**: Ready to post
6. **POSTED**: Live on TikTok!

### Adding a Script to Pipeline
- From Content Studio: click **Send to Pipeline** after generating
- From Script Library: click the send icon on any saved script
- From Script of the Day: click **Accept & Add to Pipeline**

### Managing the Pipeline
1. Go to **Video Pipeline** in the sidebar
2. You'll see a board with columns for each status
3. Drag cards between columns, or click to change status
4. Click any card to see full details, add notes, or assign

## 4. Winners Bank

Winners are your best-performing videos. FlashFlow tracks them to make future scripts even better.

### Importing Winners
1. Go to **Winners Bank** → **Import**
2. Paste TikTok video URLs of your best performers
3. FlashFlow extracts the hook, metrics, and patterns

### Analyzing Patterns
1. Go to **Winners Bank** → **Patterns**
2. Click **Run Analysis** — the AI will analyze all your winners
3. You'll see:
   - Top hook types (what openings work best)
   - Best content formats
   - Winning formulas
   - Recommendations for future content

### How Winners Improve Your Scripts
- When you generate scripts, the AI automatically checks your Winners Bank
- It uses proven hooks and patterns from your winners
- **Script of the Day** will remix winner hooks with new products
- You'll see "Based on winner: [hook]" when this happens

## 5. Content Calendar & Scheduling

### Content Calendar
1. Go to **Content Calendar** in the sidebar
2. See your scheduled content on a weekly/monthly view
3. Click any day to add content manually

### Auto-Fill
1. Click **Auto-Fill Week** button
2. FlashFlow distributes your APPROVED videos across the week
3. Rules:
   - Max 3 videos per account per day
   - No duplicate products on the same day
   - Round-robin across your TikTok accounts

### Posting Queue
- Videos move here when scheduled for posting
- See what's going out today, tomorrow, this week

## 6. For VAs (Video Editors)

### VA Dashboard
VAs access their work at `/va` (separate from the admin area).

### What VAs See
1. **Available Work** — videos waiting to be claimed
2. **My Active Videos** — videos they're currently editing
3. **Editing Brief** — for each video, a detailed brief with:
   - Full script with timestamps
   - Product info and reference images
   - Style notes and requirements
   - Quality checklist

### VA Workflow
1. Go to Available Work
2. Click **Claim** on a video
3. Edit the video following the brief
4. Click **Submit for Review** when done
5. Wait for approval (you'll be notified)
6. If revisions needed, you'll see notes on what to change

## 7. Script of the Day

Your daily AI-recommended script to film.

1. Go to **Script of the Day** in the sidebar (first item!)
2. See today's recommended script with:
   - Product chosen based on what needs content most
   - AI-generated script with hook and beats
   - Filming checklist: props, location, lighting tips
   - Why this script was chosen
3. **Accept & Add to Pipeline** if you like it
4. **Regenerate** if you want a different one
5. View previous days' scripts below

## 8. Tips & Tricks

### Keyboard Shortcuts
- **Cmd/Ctrl + K** — Universal search (find anything instantly)
- **G then D** — Go to Dashboard
- **G then S** — Go to Content Studio
- **G then P** — Go to Pipeline
- **G then W** — Go to Winners
- **G then C** — Go to Calendar

### Bulk Operations
- On the Pipeline or Products page, click checkboxes to select multiple items
- Use bulk actions: assign, change status, delete, export

### Quick Search
- Press **Cmd/Ctrl + K** from anywhere
- Type to search products, scripts, videos, or navigate to any page

### Content Package Generator
- Go to **Content Package** in the sidebar
- Click **Generate Package** — creates 20 scripts at once
- Only keeps scripts scoring 7+/10
- Automatically picks products that need content most

### Dark/Light Mode
- Click the theme toggle in the header to switch
