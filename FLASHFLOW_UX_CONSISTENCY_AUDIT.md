# FlashFlow UX Consistency Audit

Second-pass review after the dashboard refactor. Intent: make the entire FlashFlow app feel like one product with one voice.

## How this pass scoped itself

Given the size of the codebase (78 files reference the jargon terms, plus deep feature areas), this audit focused on **what a typical creator actually touches in their first week**:

- Dashboard home
- Script creation (Content Studio)
- Video library / pipeline
- Calendar / planning
- Top ideas (winners-bank)
- Bottom navigation + "More" drawer
- Core action labels (used across every video card)

Admin-only, internal, and command-center screens were left alone — they're not in the creator's path.

## Area-by-area audit

### 1. Dashboard (`/admin/dashboard`) — REVIEWED & FIXED

**Issues found:**
- Headline "What's next, brandon?" was punchy but not useful
- "Your content command center" was enterprise software jargon
- "Scripts / Campaigns / Posted" stat labels read as raw counts, not useful metrics
- "Production Pipeline" was backend language
- "Your next moves" / "Up Next" / "Winning Content" / "Quick Access" were all slightly off
- Empty states showed "No winners yet. Post content!" — exclamation-marks + unclear verbs

**Changes made:**
- Headline shifted to friendly coach tone
- Stat cards now read as labeled actions ("Scripts written", "Content plans", "Videos posted")
- Sections renamed to plain descriptive phrases ("What to do next", "Where your videos are", "Up next today", "Top ideas right now", "Shortcuts")
- Empty states now explain what, why, and what to do
- Subheads added under section titles where intent wasn't obvious

### 2. Create a video (`/admin/content-studio`) — REVIEWED & PARTIALLY FIXED

**Changes made:**
- H1: "Content Studio" → "Create a video"
- Subhead: "Write scripts for any content type — pick a format, add your product, and go" → "Pick a format, add your product, and we'll write the script."
- Welcome banner: "Welcome to Content Studio" → "Welcome — let's make your first video"
- Login gate message: "Please log in to access Content Studio." → "Please log in to create a video."

**Remaining follow-up:**
- Secondary header buttons still say "Talk Through It", "What should I make?", "Hooks", "Content Pack", "Library" — review for tone consistency (low-priority, they're reasonable)
- The "Write Script" CTA in the welcome banner body text still capitalized — should be "Write script" in sentence case

### 3. Videos / Pipeline (`/admin/pipeline`) — HEADER-ONLY FIX

**Changes made:**
- Empty-state heading: "Your Production Board" → "Your videos live here"
- Empty-state body rewritten in plain English
- Primary CTA: "Generate a Script" → "Write a script"
- Secondary CTA: "Create Video" → "Create a video"

**Remaining follow-up:**
- The pipeline page is 3,400+ lines and contains many in-context buttons still using legacy verbs (e.g. "Create Content Item", "View in Planner"). These are secondary actions and not blocking; queue for a dedicated pipeline-page pass.
- `getStatusConfig` and `formatStatusLabel` in `/lib/status` should be checked against the new pill labels ("In editing", "Ready to post").

### 4. Plan (`/admin/calendar`) — HEADER FIX

- Subhead: "Plan, schedule, and manage your content pipeline" → "Schedule your videos and map out the week ahead."

**Remaining follow-up:**

### 5. Top ideas (`/admin/intelligence/winners-bank`) — HEADER FIX

**Changes made:**

## First-time-user final pass

Simulating a brand-new TikTok Shop affiliate who just signed up, landed on the dashboard, and is holding a phone:

| Moment | Prior experience | New experience |
|---|---|---|
| Lands on dashboard | "What's next, brandon? Your content command center" — is this software for me? | "Hey brandon — let's keep it moving. Here's what needs your attention today." — feels like a person |
| Sees stat cards | "Scripts: 0. Campaigns: 0. Posted: 0." — campaigns? | "Scripts written: 0. Content plans: 0. Videos posted: 0." — clear |
| Scans sections | Production Pipeline, Up Next, Winning Content, Quick Access — reads like an app menu | Where your videos are, Up next today, Top ideas right now, Shortcuts — reads like a checklist |
| Taps bottom nav | Studio? Planner? Winners? | Create, Videos, Plan, Ideas, More |
| Taps Create | Lands on "Content Studio" screen | Lands on "Create a video" screen |
| Empty state on Up Next | "No videos in the queue." | "Nothing in your queue yet. Start a video and it'll show up here." + Create a video button |
| Taps a video needing caption | "Generate Post Package" | "Get it ready to post" |

**Residual friction flagged:** when a user taps "Ideas" in the bottom nav they land on a page with sub-labels like "Score", "Format", "CTA tag" — these are still analyst-y. Queue for a Top Ideas content pass.
